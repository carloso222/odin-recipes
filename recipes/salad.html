Chicken salad


Description

The Chicken Salad is a timeless classic, celebrated for its perfect balance of textures and its incredible versatility. At its core, it is a dish that harmonizes lean protein with a creamy base, often elevated by a signature crunch.


Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken breast (shredded or cubed)
  • Mayonnaise (as the creamy base)
  • Celery (finely chopped for crunch)
  • Canned peas and carrots (drained)
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Shred the cooked chicken breast into a large bowl.
  2. Mix in the finely chopped celery and the drained peas and carrots.
  3. Add the mayonnaise, sour cream, and mustard (if using)Add the mayonnaise, sour cream, and mustard (if using)
  4. Stir all ingredients together until everything is evenly coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for 20 minutes before serving for better flavor.
