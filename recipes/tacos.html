Tacos



Description

Tacos al Pastor are a beloved Mexican classic featuring thinly sliced marinated pork. The meat is seasoned with a blend of dried chilies, spices, and achiote, then slow-roasted on a vertical spit called a trompo. Served on warm corn tortillas, they are traditionally topped with fresh onions, cilantro, and a signature slice of roasted pineapple for the perfect balance of savory and sweet.


Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Corn tortillas
  • Achiote marinade
  • Pineapple
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Marinate: Blend the chilies, achiote, and spices to make a paste. Coat the pork slices and let them rest.
  2. Stack: Layer the meat and pineapple slices (traditionally on a vertical spit, but you can use a skewer or pan)
  3. Roast: Slow-cook the meat until the edges are crispy and browned.
  4. Slice: Thinly carve the meat and the roasted pineapple.
  5. Assemble: Serve on warm corn tortillas and top with onion, cilantro, and lime.
